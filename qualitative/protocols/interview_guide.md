# Interview Guide (Pilot)

## Groups
- Security agents (PM / Municipal Security)
- Public managers
- Street outreach / social assistance
- Users (with ethical procedures)

## Core Topics
1) Perceived displacement after operations (where/why)
2) Adaptation strategies (routes, hiding, timing)
3) Relationship with services (health/social)
4) Collateral effects (violence, loss of belongings, fear)
5) What would reduce harm and stabilize the territory
